## About Me

I'm **Ranjith Kumar Peddi** — a Principal Software Engineer and Engineering Lead with **18+ years of IT experience**, including **10+ years** deep in the Data Center industry at **Equinix**, a global network-neutral colocation leader.

I operate at the intersection of **Customer Experience**, **Digital Service Operations**, and **Data Center ecosystems** — building intelligent, scalable systems across case management, omnichannel support, order fulfilment, and cross-platform integration. My work blends hands-on engineering with business architecture, and increasingly, **AI-driven automation and analytics**.

Currently based in **Frisco, TX**, I bring a rare combination of enterprise-grade technical delivery and domain expertise that spans colocation, DCIM, edge computing, and AI — from system design to production release.

- 🔭 **Currently working on** AI-driven automation for enterprise service ecosystems
- 📝 **I write about** technology, architecture & innovation on [Medium](https://medium.com/@peddi.ranjithk) and [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=InqNJRsAAAAJ&hl=en)
- 🌐 **Connect with me** on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ranjith-kumar-peddi-87929223/)
- ⚡ **Fun fact:** I've architected solutions that power billions of dollars in data center operations globally
- 🎯 **Passion:** Bridging the gap between complex engineering challenges and elegant business solutions
